abilitec Bundle

Acxioms patented linking technology brings together disparate representations of names and addresses, enabling a single, current, and accurate view of each person or postal address. Unlike other technologies that utilize comparative matching to bring together only similar customer records, Acxioms proprietary linking technology draws upon a knowledge base to link all customer records quickly and accurately bringing together similar and dissimilar representations, and then assigning one link for each record to allow the most complete view of each customer. In person documents, the abilitec bundle returns AbiliTec consumer links. In place documents, the abilitec bundle returns AbiliTec address links. In entity documents, the abilitec bundle returns AbiliTec address and/or consumer links, depending on the type of entity represented. Documents returned by the portrait endpoint or portrait documents returned from the match endpoint (e.g., as a result of not finding an acceptable match from the persisted documents) do not contain the abilitec bundle.
